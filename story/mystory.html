"Go-To" Places in Prince Edward County

Here are some of my favourite places in Prince Edward County! Make a day of it and tour the County
Try all the tasty food and beverages the County has to offer!
All wineries are defined with a pink icon, beaches are symbolized with blue and yellow are all other activities!
Norman Hardie Winery
Wine, pizza and oysters are sure to draw a crowd everyday in the summer. My favourite Chardonnay in the County
Hillier Estates Winery
Great spot for pictures, stone fire pizza and Wine
RedTail Winery
Great atmosphere and best rose
Waupoos Estates & Winery
BEST vegetarian burger around, and offer ciders, wine and spirits
Prince Eddies Brewing Company
Great atmosphere, live music, amazing food and offers beer and ciders
Lavender fields
Must see spot from late June through July, offering a myriad of colours, scents and tastes
North Beach
Over 1km of sandy beach to enjoy
Sandbanks Provincial Park
The park is considered one best sandy beaches in Ontario, birdwatching, camping, cycling, fishing, and swimming are among the activities available in the park.
Mustang Drive In
Drive In movies and tasty snacks in the heart of Prince Edward County, Ontario. The Mustang Drive-In has been around since 1954 and it's still going strong!
Oeno Gallery
the Oeno Gallery exhibits contemporary art by artists from this region;
